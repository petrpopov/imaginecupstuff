1) You can make your protectors blow the ennemi AI by getting its position by the OtherNanoBots collection (if the AI is being seen by one of your bots) and order your protectors to "hunt" him.

2) The AI don't move to other hoshimi point because it's hardcoded that it just go to two.

3) Use Utils.MaxBot to get the maximum number of bot you can create.

4) For the moment I've got 880 points so it's possible to have 300+ ;) Improving your IA will improve your score, that what the competition is all about :p
