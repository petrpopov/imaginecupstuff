  U could simply examine the map at the beginning of the game and find the location of all the points (Hoshimi,AZN ... PierreTeamInjectionPoint ... mission objectives ...) and ... U can also find the type of each point of the map (Low/medium/high density .... in stream ...). U have to do this for round 2 kuz U won't know the map (meaning U can't hard-code the location of the points of the map but U can use the tissue on which the game is taking place to extract the locations of the points that interest you). U can find in other posts on the forum how to get the locations from the map.

  U don't know where every ennemy bot is on the map, of course, except the ones that are in your range of sight (they'll be accesible through the OtherNanoBotsInfo and U can get this way their position so U could eventually shoot them or avoid them).
