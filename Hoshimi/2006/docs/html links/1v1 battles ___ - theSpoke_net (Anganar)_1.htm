If there are objectives in the 1v1 battles, they will probably be for each team, so that if one team has more points but did not complete the objective, it will loose.

Also, there may be 1v1 specific objectives, for example: "Collect more AZN then the opponent" would be the default one. How about: "Kill more WC then you opponent", that one would be nice, or even: "Get to these 5 locations on the map before your opponent".

About permission to kill your opponents bot, I think that it could be allowed or not, depending on the objectives. Or even it may be allowed only to kill certain types of bots, like observers and blockes.
